# AION: Algorithmic Reversal of Genomic Entropy - Formal Whitepaper

## 1. Introduction
This whitepaper formally details the computational models underlying the AION project, specifically focusing on the treatment of biological aging as an information-theoretic problem.

## 2. Methodology
We utilize a discrete-time cellular automata model combined with a boolean network representation of Genetic Regulatory Networks (GRNs).

## 3. Findings
Initial simulations indicate that the application of specific reset vectors (analogous to Yamanaka factors) effectively reverses entropy accumulation.

## 4. Conclusion
Aging can be formally modeled and computationally reversed.
