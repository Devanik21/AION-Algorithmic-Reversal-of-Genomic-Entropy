# Experiment Design: Evaluating Algorithmic Reversal of Entropy

## Objective
To quantify the efficacy of discrete algorithmic resets (Yamanaka factors) on the overall Shannon Entropy of a simulated Genetic Regulatory Network (GRN) over extended epochs.

## Variables
- **Independent Variable:** Frequency and intensity of Yamanaka factor application.
- **Dependent Variable:** Total GRN Information Entropy ($H$).
- **Control:** Population with identical mutation rate and no algorithmic reset.
